Curly Hair Care

Are you ready to give your curly hair the routine it needs?Are you ready to begin the path to strong, healthy, moisturized hair? If yes, follow these instructions below!!

What you need:
  1. Detangling brush
  2. Natural shampoo and conditioner
  3. A good leave in conditioner
  4. Hair/scalp oil
  5. Styling cream/curl cream
  6. Mousse
  7. Holding gel
  8. Patience and love for your hair!

Good brands to consider:
  • Mielle
  • Carol's Daughter
  • Miss Jessie's
  • Camille Rose
  • As I Am

Using these products will just be the beginning of your journey to achieving your goal of healthy hair! By dedicating time and patience to using these products properly, you will successfully give your hair what it needs to thrive! :)
